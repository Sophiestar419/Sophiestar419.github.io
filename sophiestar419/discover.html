IRT Presenation Reflection

For our IRT presentation, our group presented on the Rubik's cube and the cognitive functions behind the cube - psychologically, metnally, and genetically. In terms of our presenation, I would say that our TED talk style where we talked a bit more informally, as well as posed questions to the audience to amp up the interactivness, was super fun! It was much better than a lecture-style presenation, and I quite enjoyed it. We all did super well, and overall didn't get too much critisicm from the audience. In terms of the future, I wish we could've prepped the presentation a bit more. I especially felt that the demo that I tried to do fell a bit flat, and I wish I could've had more time to plan it out, even though I think it succeeded in the end. I would also love to steal the sacred architechure group's final slide about the "so what?" of their presenation. It was a super susinct and effective way to wrap up their presentation, and I quite liked it.
