Sophia's Capstone Project

A research project on the media and the prevalence of misinformation

My LEAP

For my LEAP, I'll be researching how misinformation in the media has affected the larger public and skewed perceptions of what is true and what is not. As someone whose family has quite a divide in the political and social ideologies we posess, it often frustrates me to see fmaily members use factually incorrect news as truth without knowing how to tell if the sources they're using are fake news. Through this research, I'll be hoping to use machine learning and AI to develop an app that can be personalized to a user in helping to detect fake news.
